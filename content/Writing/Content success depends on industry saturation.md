The ultimate type of content for you depends a lot on the industry, and to what degree there is content saturation already. As an example, the article proposes, doing ultimate guides and list content might be a really effective content piece, or feel extremely tired and uninspired, all depending on how saturated the industry they work in are with such content.

> Overengineering your content strategy is a waste of resources. 	Be mindful of your market and industry.
